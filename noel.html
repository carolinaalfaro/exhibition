Coding the Past


Earthquake Essay

Click Image to see the rest

For English, I wrote an essay based on a word I took from my interview transcript I did to my interviewee, the word was “earthquake”. I came up with my big idea by realizing that many people take their life for granted and never fully taking time to cherish the things around them or spend enough time with the people that love them. While that time, a natural disaster like this can occur and take the lives away of many who might of not appreciate the life they were living in enough.I weaved in the voices of others by adding a quote that comes from a source on the internet. It explains how earthquakes occur often and not just when you feel them. It also talks about how they come in all different magnitudes.

My layer subject portrait resembles what I wanted to put on my layer subject portriat. It also talks about how I placed things were they are right now. My composition process on how I laid out the images on the canvas was by putting all the earthquake or historical event photos as a mountain shaped on the bottom width of my canvas, I kinda think of it as a border-bottom. The reason I place them like this because I wanted the earthquake pictures at the bottom showing that there like the past and above them is the present which are the photos of her adventures.
